Limber Pine Adventure for Families

The summer is hot at Bear Lake and while the cool water is refreshing, there's more than one way to escape the heat. One of the local favorite hikes is also friendly with the littles. Just a little over 7 miles west of Garden City along route 89 is the Limber Pine nature trail.

At just over 1.5-miles round trip, the Limber Pine nature trail is perfect for families. Along this interpretive trail you'll find education stations that teach about local flora and fauna, all while you take in sweeping views of Bear Lake below you.

The final treat of this hike is a giant 560-year-old limber pine with a 25-ft diameter. It's the perfect place for a summer picnic. Just remember, this is bear country, so make sure your kids only share their treats and snacks with each other and not the wildlife, or you'll have a little more adventure than you planned for.

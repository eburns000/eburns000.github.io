Lightning Strikes Sherman Peak

Last night the trail to Sherman Peak lit up with quite the lightning storm. Fortunately, because of the extra moisture we experienced this past week, no fires were started, but the park service warns any hikers this weekend to be watchful for any downed tree limbs.

One of our local photographers here in Preston, Al Mafoto, just happened to have his camera with him last night and captured the excitement of the storm. He told Sherman Peak Weather reporters that “in the 20 years I’ve lived in Preston, I’ve never seen a storm light up the sky quite like last night.”

While the weekend sky is expected to remain clear, the weather forecast for next week shows a high likelihood of more action in the sky. So, get your camera out and see if you can catch the storm light up the sky, at a safe distance, of course.

For more information on trail conditions on the Bear River Ridge, be sure to check back on our site – trail conditions are updated daily. Hike safely!

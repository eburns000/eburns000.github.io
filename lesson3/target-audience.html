Target Audience

The target audience is somewhat broad, including men and women, age 18-45, who are adventure seekers, outdoor enthusiasts, hikers and/or mountain climbers. The Bear River Range has several trails and climbs that will appeal to varied levels of skill – from the non-technical climb to the summit of Sherman Peak to several areas of rugged cliffs accessible through the Highline National Recreation Trail. The site needs to address those interested in hiking trails that are suited to families as well as options for more technical climbs throughout the range.

Personas

  • Name: Russel
  • Age: 25
  • Marital Status: Single
  • Income: $45,000/year

Russel is a native of Southeast Idaho and loves the outdoors and mountain climbing. He’s already familiar with many of the trails and climbs in the Bear River Range, but he needs to know the current weather and trail conditions for his favorite climbs and seeks updates on weather so he can plan for his climb for the upcoming weekend. He also enjoys seeing updates of other climbers, reviews, and pictures of climbs they have made and submitted to the site and learning new routes. He would be accessing the site using his iPhone 6, because he cannot afford the latest iPhone, but values smartphone technology. He would be viewing the site while out with his friends in the evening time on a weeknight, planning which trails and hikes to take the weekend, checking the weather to make sure they’ll be able to have a safe climb.

  • Name: Kimberliee
  • Age: 35
  • Marital Status: Married
  • Income: $65,000/year

Kimberlie lives in Ogden, UT with her family and is vacationing in Bear Lake with her extended family at one of the many lodges. She is sitting on the sofa in the early morning before the kids are awake, looking for hikes and trails she can take her young family on in the area later that day. She wants to make sure the weather will cooperate and that the trails will be safe and clear. She enjoys the outdoors and taking her family on excursions but is not a technical climber and is looking for easy family hikes. She also wants to know where the trailhead is and how long it will take to get there. She would be accessing the site using her laptop.

Scenarios

  • What is the weather going to be like this weekend for the climb I want to make?
  • I need to know if the trailhead for a certain peak is open this time of year.
  • What trailheads are closest to Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven?
  • What are some recent experiences of hikers in the Bear River Range?
  • What hiking trails in the Bear River Range are best for families with young children?
  • What does the view look like from the top of Sherman Peak?
  • How do I submit a review of a trail I just went on?

Site Name

Sherman Peak Weather and Trail

Site Purpose

Sherman Peak is the highest point in the Bear River Range in Southeast Idaho. The Bear River Range is the backyard outdoor playground for hiking and climbing enthusiasts of those residing or vacationing in the surrounding communities including Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. The purpose of this site is to provide weather and trail information to climbers and hikers including local climbing enthusiasts and those vacationing in the area, including the popular Bear Lake.

Primary Objectives of the Site

  • Provide weather for all three communities surrounding the Bear River Range, including Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven, Idaho.
  • Provide information on various hiking and climbing options for people visiting the area.
  • Provide updates on routes and trails that may be affected adversely affected by the weather, such as hazards or closures.
  • Allow users of the site to share recent climbing or hiking experiences to provide reviews of hikes or climbs and to give visitors to the area a sense of what the range has to offer.

Domain Name

Typography

The fonts selected from thsi website were chosen

Heading Font: Anton

The heading font is a sans-serif font and was selected to stand out in contrast from the paragraphy font. It was selected to communicate a sense of adventure.

Paragraph Font: Signika Light

The paragraph font, also a sans-serif font, was selected to be readable on any screen size, but especially smaller viewports, and to pair well with the Anton font.

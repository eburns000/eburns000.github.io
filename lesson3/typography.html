Typography

The fonts selected for this website are Google fonts.

Heading Font: Anton

The Anton font is a sans-serif font and was selected to stand out in contrast from the paragraph font. It was selected to communicate a sense of adventure.

Paragraph Font: Signika Light

The Signika font, also a sans-serif font, was selected to be readable on any screen size, but especially with smaller viewports, and to pair well with the Anton font.

Color Scheme

The primary color for this website is blue. The color blue (specifically #355EAB) was chosen because it represents assurance, trust, and confidence, and those traits are what visitors to this site value as they seek for weather forecasts and trail conditions. Setting the primary color to blue (#355EAB), the accent colors were derived using the triad and complementary color rules to come up with accent colors. The following colors will be used for this site.

Primary (blue): [#355EAB]

  • h1, h2, h3, h4, h5, h6
  • call to action background
  • button border and text; fill on hover

Accent (red): [#AB5546]

  • nav background on hover

Accent (green): [#495E19]

  • secondary info background color

Accent (gold): [#AB8635]

  • nav background

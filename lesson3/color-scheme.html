Color Scheme

The primary color for this website is blue. The color blue (specifically #355EAB) was chosen because it represents assurance, trust, and confidence, and those traits are what visitors to this site value as they seek for weather forecasts and trail conditions. Setting the primary color to blue (#355EAB), I used the triad and complementary color rules to come up with accent colors. The following colors will be used for this site.

Primary (blue): [#355EAB]

  • h1, h2 on white background
  • call to action paragraph background
  • nav background
  • button normal border and text; fill on hover

Accent (red): [#AB5546]

  • nav background on hover

Accent (green): [#495E19]

  • secondary info background color

Accent (gold): [#AB8635]

  • h3, h4, h5, h6 on white background

Background (snow): [#FFFAFA]

  • main content background color
  • h1, h2, h3, h4, h5, h6 on color background
  • p on color background
  • nav link color
  • button normal fill; text on hover

Normal Text (gray): [#444444]

  • p on white background

Warning Text (red): [#cc3300]

  • warning paragraph text on all background colors

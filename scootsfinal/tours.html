Suggested Places to Visit and Explore

There are many places to explore on the island, but we put together some of our favorites to get you some ideas of what the island can offer for your adventure.

Popular Beaches

You can't go wrong exploring the beaches of Cozumel, some of the most pristine beaches and water in the world. Still, these are some of our staff's favorite beaches to explore, and we believe you will find them to be a paradise dream come true.

  • Playa San Juan Beach
  • Playa Santa Pilar
  • Playa San Francisco
  • Playa Santa Rosa

San Miguel de Cozumel, the Island's Capital

Of course you likely have already come through San Miguel de Cozumel, but we have to point it out because you can't leave Cozumel without spending some time here. San Miguel de Cozumel is the island's capital, filled with opportunites for dancing, music, entertainment, shopping and dining. If you want suggestion for where to eat, just ask our front desk staff for their favorites.

Laguna Chanknaab and the National Marine Park

Small fresh water lake south of San Miguel de Cozumel in the National Marine Park The lagoon is part of the Chankanaab Beach Adventure Park

San Gervasio, Cozumel's Mayan Hidden Treasure

San Gervasio was built in AD 800 and is 16 kilometers from San Miguel de Cozumel. There you will see the Temple of Ix-chel, which was a central part of the Mayan empire.

Museum of the Island of Cozumel

Learn about the history of the island and its people from ancient artifacts and visual displays. A highlight of the museum is the exhibit showcasing the marine life around Cozumel. The museum also hosts several art exhibits, both local and international.

Punta Sur Eco Beach Park

Cozumel's largest eco park, located at the southern tip of the island, is Punta Sur Eco Beach Park. The park offers many options for exploring and playing, including scuba diving, swimming, snorkeling and kayaking. The park is over 240 acres so there is plenty to do and areas to explore.

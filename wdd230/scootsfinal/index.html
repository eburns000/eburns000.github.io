Explore Paradise

Come to Paradise and Explore with Us

Scoots Cozumel is the premier scooter, jeep, and ATV agency on Cozumel, with two locations both at Playa and at the Port. We have the best selection of scooters, side-by-side ATVs and jeeps available to rent. Our staff are knowledgable fo the island and will help you find your own adventure, whether you are wanting to ride just for the afternoon or two take a multi-day excurision on some of the less traveled parts of the island. We're here to make your vacation a dream come true.

Take a look at what we have to offer.

Scooters

Scooters are a great way to explore the beaches and the shops around Cozumel. It's the way of the island.

Side-by-Sides

For a little more adventure on some of the back trails or on the more difficult to get to beaches, check out one of our side-by-sides.

Jeeps

For the ulitmate island adventure, you will want to take out a jeep. There are some places that only a jeep can get you there. If your passion is for the unexplored territory, check out one of our jeeps for an adventure of a lifetime.
